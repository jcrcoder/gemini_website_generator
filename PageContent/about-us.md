## History

In January 2012, Juan Ramirez moved to Bogota, Colombia – both to escape Chicago winters and to start a company that could outsource design and development better than the other offerings on the market.

Juan built out a talent pool across Chicago and Colombia and it was a perfect match: same time zones, great engineers and technologists, and a range of shared interests.

NSerio comes from a Spanish saying that means "seriously" – and we're serious about our commitment to our customers and making sure our team represents the great intellectual capacity both Colombia and Chicago have to offer.

## Our Values

We operate by a set of core values that drive our decisions and how we work with our customers.

**Customer Success First:** our top priority isn't just delivering a great product, but making sure our customers are successful with it once it's completed.

**Love Your Work:** we thrive on challenges and love solving problems with technology.

**Quality over Speed:** deadlines are important, but we never sacrifice building the right solution to rush it out the door.

**Service Mindset:** we're an extension of your team and here for you, even if scope changes or new requirements arise.

**Confront New Challenges Head-on:** while we've seen a lot, we're never scared to tackle a new problem and get creative with our solutions.
